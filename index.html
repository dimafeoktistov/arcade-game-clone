Frogger Game!

To win in this game you should collect 3 diamonds and avoid bugs. To start plaing press "Start game" button. To move around use your arrow buttons.

When you will win or lose in game you will get additional instructions.

Helper notes

You have 5 lifes so you don't need to worry about bugs too much. And anyway if you feel like you are loosing you always can press restart button.

Also you can use keyboard shortcut for restarting which is: R
